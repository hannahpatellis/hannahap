The profoundly emotional moments, memories, and experiences of life as a trans woman in the American South are some of the main focuses in my work. Inspired from a young age by abstract contemporary art, I have always gravitated toward color and movement captured in pieces that leave much interpretation open to the viewer. While my primary medium is digital still visual art, I do dabble in both motion media and physical media. Vivid colors, fluid motion, and deep dimensionality are present in much of my work. My goal in creating art is to empower people to express themselves and use art as a launchpad for introspection.

I am open for commissions and showings!
