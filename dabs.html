Dossier Access for Buyers System

Mission: Create a client interface for reorders, a digital catalogue, and customer management system for wholesale customers of Glassheads Wholesale

DEVELOPMENT: PHP + MYSQL

The Dossier Access for Buyers System (DABS) is a web-based portal for wholesale clients that allows clients to check pending orders, place new orders, and shop wholesale catalogs all from a company-supplied Android tablet.

A common problem with the wholesale company was that placing orders and handling accounts could be a hassle. Having to call a rep and wait for a return call proved to be inefficient. By providing a tablet with an easy way to place orders made it easy for both the wholesale company and the wholesale buyers. In addition to their assigned agent, they had full access to their company information and ordering without ever having to pick up the phone.

Technologies used

  • PHP
  • MySQL
  • Shopify integration
  • Bootstrap 3
